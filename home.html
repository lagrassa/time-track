Tooling Tracker

Figure out if your classes are actually 12 units

How it Works

Punt/Tool Distribution

We run a Window Analysis tool that uses regular expressions to find the name of your window. Depending on apps that you determine to be punting (like Facebook) or tooling (a terminal), you can keep track of what time you spend punting and tooling, and watch the ratio on a graph.

Current Time Distribution

For activities that don't take place on a computer, use our Android application to time when you start activities for each class. You can log how much time you spend on each class this way, and determine how many hours per week you spend on it.
